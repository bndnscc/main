Drill Style?

Drill style of trap music that originated in the South Side, Chicago, in early 2010.
It is defined by its dark, violent and nihilistic lyrical content and ominous tap-influenced beats.
Artists within the genre have been noted for their style of lyricism and association with crime in Chicago.
